# Chatbot Test Cases & Example Responses

Use these to test that the chatbot responds appropriately.

---

## Body Image & Self-Worth

| User Says | Expected: Caring, affirming response |
|-----------|--------------------------------------|
| I feel I am ugly | Affirms worth, beauty is more than appearance, "you matter" |
| I hate how I look | Same—validates feelings, affirms inner beauty |
| I think I'm unattractive | Warm, supportive, "you have value" |
